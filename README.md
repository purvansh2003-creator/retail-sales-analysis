# 📊 Retail Sales Data Analysis

## 📌 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing a retail sales dataset using Python and Pandas to extract meaningful business insights. The goal is to understand sales performance, profitability, and identify areas of improvement.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze total sales and profit
* Identify top-performing regions and cities
* Evaluate profitability across categories and sub-categories
* Detect loss-making products and orders
* Explore high sales but low profit scenarios

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* GroupBy aggregation (Region, Category, City, Sub-Category)
* Filtering (high sales, loss-making orders)
* Feature engineering (Profit per item)
* Order-level and product-level analysis

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

1. The West region generates the highest sales, indicating strong market performance.
2. Technology is the most profitable category, while some sub-categories like Tables incur losses.
3. Certain cities contribute significantly to total sales, showing uneven revenue distribution.
4. Some orders have high sales but result in negative profit, suggesting pricing or discount issues.
5. High-value products are typically sold in lower quantities but contribute significantly to revenue.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Focus on reducing losses in sub-categories like Tables.
* Review pricing and discount strategies to prevent loss-making high-sales orders.
* Optimize product mix to improve profitability.

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analysis can help identify profitable areas and uncover hidden losses, enabling better business decision-making.
